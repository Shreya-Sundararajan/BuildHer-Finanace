About Us

Nakshatra & Shreya are 12th-grade students committed to advancing equity and empowerment among underrepresented women globally. We believe that awareness, digital access, and storytelling can drive positive change. Voice2Learn is our initiative to turn global gender inequality into relatable human stories, driving awareness and action.
